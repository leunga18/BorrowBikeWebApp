BorrowBike
About
BorrowBike is University of Portland's on-campus bike rental system.
It was created as a Senior Design project to streamline the existing bike rental system.
Just swipe your student ID card and go!

Policies and Guildlines
  • Renter assumes full financial responsibility for bike and helmet
  • Theft or loss of any items = full replacement or repair expense
  • Bikes are for short-term use (72 hours)
  • Bikes must be locked accordingly
